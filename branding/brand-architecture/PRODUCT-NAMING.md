# Sky365 Product Naming Standard

## Canonical Names

- Sky365
- Sky365 Core
- Sky365 Enterprise
- Sky365 AI
- Sky365 Agentic
- Skyverse
- Skyverse by Sky365

## Prohibited Variants

Do not use:

- Sky 365
- SKY 365
- Sky3DS5
- Skyburst
- Skyverse 365 unless approved as a campaign name
- Agent Edition without the Sky365 master brand

## Recommended Public Presentation

| Layer | Display Name | Descriptor |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Sky365 Core | Modular Monolith ERP |
| 2 | Sky365 Enterprise | Scale, Governance & Integration |
| 3 | Sky365 AI | Intelligence & Automation Layer |
| 4 | Sky365 Agentic | Autonomous Agents & Digital Workforce |
| 5 | Skyverse by Sky365 | Metadata-Driven Agentic Platform |

## Endorsement Rule

Until Skyverse has independent market recognition, use **Skyverse by Sky365** on websites, proposals, videos and campaign assets.

## Capitalization

- Use `Sky365`, not `SKY365`, in normal prose.
- `SKY365` may be used in compact all-caps labels or logo lockups.
- Use `Skyverse` with a capital S only.
- Use `Agentic` as a product-layer name only when preceded by Sky365.

## File Naming

Use lowercase kebab-case:

```text
sky365-core-logo.svg
sky365-enterprise-card.png
sky365-ai-banner.webp
sky365-agentic-motion.mp4
skyverse-by-sky365-lockup.svg
```
