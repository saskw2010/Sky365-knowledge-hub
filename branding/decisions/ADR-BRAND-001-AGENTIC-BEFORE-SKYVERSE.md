# ADR-BRAND-001 — Position Sky365 Agentic Before Skyverse

- **Status:** Accepted as current branding decision
- **Date:** 2026-07-20
- **Owner:** Mostafa Elnagar
- **Scope:** Sky365 brand and product evolution

## Context

The product family needs to communicate a credible progression from ERP operations to enterprise governance, intelligence, autonomous execution and finally a metadata-driven connected platform.

A four-stage model merged Agentic into Skyverse. That model was simpler, but it hid an important commercial and technical transition: the move from AI assistance to autonomous digital workforce execution.

## Decision

Use the following five-stage product evolution:

```text
Sky365 Core → Sky365 Enterprise → Sky365 AI → Sky365 Agentic → Skyverse
```

Sky365 Agentic is positioned immediately before Skyverse.

## Rationale

- AI and Agentic are not the same promise.
- AI understands, recommends and automates bounded tasks.
- Agentic executes, collaborates, orchestrates tools and delivers outcomes.
- Skyverse is broader than an agent runtime: it is the metadata-driven universe that composes apps, agents, workflows, context, memory, data and experiences.

## Consequences

### Positive

- Clearer product maturity ladder.
- Stronger digital-workforce positioning.
- Easier packaging of Agentic capabilities before full Skyverse adoption.
- Better alignment between technical architecture and commercial messaging.

### Risks

- Five stages increase branding and asset-maintenance cost.
- Agentic and Skyverse can appear overlapping if messaging is weak.

### Mitigation

Use this distinction consistently:

- **Sky365 Agentic:** autonomous execution layer.
- **Skyverse:** metadata-driven platform and connected experience universe.

## Review Trigger

Review this ADR only if product packaging, sales capacity or platform architecture proves that Agentic cannot remain a separately communicated evolution layer.
