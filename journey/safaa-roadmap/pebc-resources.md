# PEBC and Pharmacy Language Resources

## Source hierarchy

1. PEBC official blueprint, exam pages, sample questions and information videos.
2. Structured preparation providers for drills and explanations.
3. Community videos only when their content matches the current official blueprint.

## YouTube Auto Dubbing rule

Auto dubbing may be used for additional French listening exposure, but it is not an authoritative translation of drug names, doses, clinical instructions or regulatory terminology.
