SKY365 EDUCATION VISION

A governed, agentic education operating system.

Sky365 can differentiate by combining adaptive learning, trusted institutional knowledge, multilingual tutoring, teacher workflow automation, ERP integration, evidence-backed analytics, and controlled AI agents in one modular platform. The goal is not to clone one competitor; it is to integrate the strongest patterns while solving enterprise, school, government, and regional deployment requirements that point products leave fragmented.

Proposed Modules

Learning Experience, Curriculum Studio, Assessment Engine, Adaptive Pathways, Content & RAG Library, Teacher Copilot, Student Tutor, Parent Portal, Classroom Operations, Skills Graph, Analytics, Intervention Center, Credentialing, Marketplace, Integration Hub.

Required AI Agents

Curriculum Agent, Lesson Designer, AI Tutor, Assessment Agent, Feedback Agent, Intervention Agent, Teacher Assistant, Parent Briefing Agent, Accessibility Agent, Translation Agent, Compliance Agent, School Operations Agent.

Integrations

Sky365 ERP, LMS/LTI, SIS, Microsoft 365, Google Workspace, identity/SSO, content repositories, video meeting tools, payment, messaging, OCR/document ingestion, speech services, government data exchanges.

MVP

Teacher workspace, curriculum-aware RAG, lesson and quiz generation, student tutor with guardrails, adaptive recommendations, basic analytics, multilingual chat, approval workflow, and export to common LMS formats.

Enterprise Version

Multi-school tenancy, SSO, private AI, role-based policy, audit, advanced integrations, skills graph, intervention workflows, custom curriculum, SLA, data residency, and executive analytics.

Government Version

National curriculum graph, sovereign deployment, Arabic-first and multilingual delivery, offline/low-bandwidth mode, district hierarchy, national assessment analytics, procurement governance, accessibility, and policy-controlled agents.

Roadmap

Phase 1 · Foundation

Education ontology, curriculum ingestion, identity, policy, content RAG, teacher workspace.

Phase 2 · MVP

Lesson planning, AI tutor, assessment, student profiles, adaptive recommendations, dashboards.

Phase 3 · Enterprise

Multi-tenant administration, integrations, private deployment, intervention agents, analytics.

Phase 4 · Government

National curriculum, sovereign AI, offline delivery, district operations, policy and evidence layer.

Strategic position: Build the governed education core and agent runtime; integrate commodity foundation models, speech, OCR, productivity suites, and selected content providers; buy only where accreditation, proprietary curriculum, or specialized assessment makes partnership faster and lower risk.
