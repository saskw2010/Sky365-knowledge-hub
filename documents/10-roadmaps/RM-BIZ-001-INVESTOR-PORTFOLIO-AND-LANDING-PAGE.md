# RM-BIZ-001 — Investor Portfolio and Landing Page Roadmap

## 1. Objective

Create an investor-facing experience that presents Sky365 as a portfolio of governed AI and enterprise-software ventures, without overstating maturity or mixing implemented products with research concepts.

## 2. Investor segmentation

### Strategic enterprise investors

Best fit for:

- Agentic ERP
- Document Intelligence
- Agent Observatory
- Cybersecurity and governance

Expected value beyond capital:

- Enterprise customers
- Distribution
- Integration access
- Compliance expertise
- Cloud and infrastructure support

### Regional technology holding companies

Best fit for:

- Gulf and MENA expansion
- Arabic-first OCR
- ERP modernization
- Government and regulated-sector opportunities

Expected value:

- Local market access
- Procurement relationships
- Regional sales capacity
- Joint ventures

### ERP and systems-integration partners

Best fit for:

- Schema-driven ERP
- Odoo and enterprise connectors
- Industry packs
- Implementation automation

Possible structures:

- Strategic investment
- Revenue-share partnership
- Joint product company
- Territory partnership
- Co-development agreement

### Cybersecurity investors and security companies

Best fit for:

- AI Security Lab
- Agent red teaming
- Prompt-injection defense
- Governance and compliance evidence

### AI infrastructure and cloud partners

Best fit for:

- Model gateway
- OCR and multimodal inference
- Agent environments
- Observability
- Secure sandboxes

### Education and training investors

Best fit for:

- School Intelligence
- Agentic Systems Academy
- Professional training products
- AI-generated course and media pipelines

### Media and creator-economy investors

Best fit for:

- Voice and Video Studio
- Avatar runtime
- Automated multilingual content production

### Angel investors and operator-investors

Best fit for early product programs with a clear pilot and focused market:

- OCR workflow
- Agent Observatory MVP
- Cybersecurity assessment product
- School pilot
- Voice/video production workflow

## 3. Investor profiles to invite

The initial invitation list should focus on people and organizations with one or more of these characteristics:

1. Enterprise software or ERP operating experience.
2. AI infrastructure, observability, or cybersecurity expertise.
3. Access to GCC, North Africa, Canada, or European enterprise customers.
4. Ability to fund a focused pilot rather than requiring a complete platform immediately.
5. Willingness to contribute distribution, data, regulatory knowledge, or technical capacity.
6. Interest in Arabic-first enterprise AI.
7. Experience with B2B SaaS and long enterprise sales cycles.

Avoid broad investor outreach before each venture has a clear use case, evidence package, capital requirement, and partnership model.

## 4. Investment portfolio structure

### Core company platform

**Sky365 Agentic Platform**

Shared assets:

- Agent Runtime
- Shared Action Core
- Policy and Approval Engine
- Semantic Layer
- Memory and Knowledge
- Trace Store and Evaluation
- Tool and Skill Registry

This layer should usually remain strategically controlled because all ventures depend on it.

### Product venture portfolio

| Venture | Stage until verified | First proof | Likely investor fit |
|---|---|---|---|
| Agentic ERP | Partial / unverified | Golden ERP module | ERP, enterprise software, regional technology |
| Document Intelligence and OCR | Incubation | Invoice or contract workflow | AI, document automation, regulated sectors |
| AI Cybersecurity Lab | Incubation | Red-team and governance assessment | Cybersecurity, compliance, enterprise |

## 5. Investment structures

The landing page should not imply that every opportunity means selling equity in the parent company.

Possible structures:

- Parent-company equity
- Product-specific subsidiary
- Joint venture
- Strategic partnership
- Pilot sponsorship
- Convertible instrument
- Revenue-share arrangement
- Territory or industry license
- Co-development agreement

Each project card must state which structures are currently considered.

## 6. Investor landing page information architecture

```text
Hero
→ Why Sky365
→ Platform foundation
→ Investment portfolio
→ Product opportunity cards
→ Evidence and traction
→ Business models
→ Partnership structures
→ Roadmap and use of funds
→ Team and operating footprint
→ Data room request
→ Contact / invitation form
```

### Hero

Suggested message:

> Build the governed enterprise-AI layer that turns documents, data, workflows, voice, and agents into safe business execution.

### Why now

Present verified market drivers without unsupported numbers:

- Enterprises are moving from chat interfaces toward action-oriented agents.
- Governance, observability, and integration are barriers to production adoption.
- Arabic and bilingual enterprise workflows remain underserved.
- Existing ERP systems contain valuable domain logic that should be adapted rather than discarded.

### Platform foundation section

Show that the ventures share one foundation:

```text
Schema and Semantic Layer
+ Agent Runtime
+ Safe Action Core
+ Knowledge and Memory
+ Security and Governance
+ Evaluation and Observability
```

### Opportunity cards

Each card must contain:

- Problem
- Product
- Target customer
- Current stage
- Existing evidence
- Next milestone
- Capital or partnership need
- Business model
- Strategic advantage
- Risks and dependencies

### Evidence and traction

Use only evidence that can be linked:

- Code repositories
- Deployed demos
- Customer or partner pilots
- Benchmarks
- Architecture decisions
- Runtime traces
- Test results
- Existing ERP modules
- Team operating history

### Use of funds

Divide by milestone rather than generic spending:

- Complete the golden ERP module
- Build OCR benchmark and pilot
- Launch Observatory MVP
- Build security attack library
- Complete one vertical pilot
- Establish sales and partner pipeline

## 7. Investor readiness score

Score each venture from 0 to 5 on:

- Problem clarity
- Customer definition
- Implementation evidence
- Technical defensibility
- Shared-platform leverage
- Go-to-market access
- Revenue model
- Regulatory readiness
- Team capability
- Capital efficiency

Do not feature a venture as “open for investment” until it has:

- Named owner
- Clear stage
- First measurable milestone
- Evidence package
- Use-of-funds plan
- Proposed investment structure
- Risk statement

## 8. Recommended first three opportunities

### 1. Agent Observatory

Why first:

- Supports every other agent product.
- Easier to demonstrate than a complete ERP transformation.
- Creates evidence, monitoring, security, and evaluation infrastructure.
- Can become a standalone B2B product.

### 2. Document Intelligence and OCR

Why second:

- Clear business pain.
- Strong Arabic/English differentiation opportunity.
- Connects directly to ERP, legal, healthcare, education, and finance.
- Can be measured with benchmark accuracy and processing economics.

### 3. AI Cybersecurity Lab

Why third:

- Trust is a prerequisite for agent adoption.
- Can begin as assessments and pilots before becoming a full platform.
- Creates strategic relationships with regulated enterprises and security partners.

Agentic ERP remains the flagship platform narrative, but it should be presented with a focused golden module rather than as a fully completed universal ERP replacement.

## 10. Delivery phases

### Phase 1 — Evidence audit

- Verify repositories and modules.
- Build project-status matrix.
- Assign venture owners.
- Select first three investment opportunities.

### Phase 2 — Investor narrative

- Finalize platform story.
- Prepare opportunity cards.
- Define capital and partnership structures.
- Create milestone-based use-of-funds plans.

### Phase 3 — Landing page

- Build responsive investor portal.
- Add portfolio filters.
- Add evidence links and demo status.
- Add controlled data-room request.

### Phase 4 — Outreach

- Strategic partner shortlist.
- Operator-investor shortlist.
- Regional investor shortlist.
- Personalized invitation packages.
- Meeting and follow-up pipeline.

## 11. Guardrail

The investor page is not a gallery of every idea discussed. It is a controlled portfolio showing which assets are platform capabilities, which are products, which are experiments, and which are open investment opportunities.
