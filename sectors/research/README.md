# Sky365 Research Sector

> The evidence-building sector that turns scattered ideas, repositories, experiments, and external research into verified product direction.

## Purpose

The Research Sector comes before investment packaging. It establishes what exists, what is missing, what is technically credible, what should be tested, and which opportunities deserve product or investment promotion.

## Core workflow

```text
Question or opportunity
→ Source discovery
→ Repository and runtime evidence
→ External research
→ Capability classification
→ Gap analysis
→ Experiment or benchmark
→ Adopt / Watch / Reject decision
→ Product recommendation
→ Investment readiness assessment
```

## Research record requirements

Every research track should record:

- Research question
- Business relevance
- Existing internal implementation evidence
- External sources
- Assumptions
- Experiment or validation method
- Findings
- Risks and limitations
- Adopt / Watch / Reject decision
- Product impact
- Investment readiness impact
- Owner and next review date

## Promotion rule

No research topic is promoted into the Investment Sector until it has:

1. A defined problem and target customer.
2. Internal evidence or a clearly scoped experiment.
3. A technical feasibility position.
4. A defensibility hypothesis.
5. A measurable next milestone.
6. A risk register.
7. An explicit recommendation.
