# Sky365 Investment Sector

> The opportunity-packaging sector for projects that have passed research and evidence gates.

## Purpose

The Investment Sector converts validated Sky365 capabilities into clear opportunities for strategic investors, operating partners, pilot sponsors, and joint ventures.

## Opportunity record requirements

Every investment opportunity must include:

- Problem
- Product
- Target market
- Customer profile
- Current maturity
- Verified evidence
- Competitive position
- Business model
- Go-to-market plan
- Next milestones
- Use of funds
- Partnership or investment structure
- Risks
- Data-room references

## Investor types

- Strategic enterprise investors
- Regional technology holdings
- ERP and system integration partners
- Cybersecurity companies
- Cloud and AI infrastructure partners
- Education and healthcare operators
- Operator angels
- Pilot sponsors
- Joint-venture partners

## Governance

A project cannot be presented as implemented unless backed by repository, runtime, customer, or pilot evidence. Research-stage opportunities must be labelled clearly as research or incubation.
